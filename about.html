Wenger the man

This is the story of Wenger. A man of great accomplishment, great wins and achievements, but without doubt, a man whose time at the Emirates has clearly come to an end. Here are some of the great things Wenger has done for Arsenal as a club

  • 3 premier League Championships
  • 2 FA Cup wins
  • Champions League Semi Finals
  • Champions League knock-out rounds every year since he took over
  • One more thing

Now that aside, let us focus on the real reason we think Wenger should be heading out the Emirates door, like yesterday.

And Wenger's many failures

Like any other coach in the premier League, Wenger does of course have his failures. And that's alright. However, what is beyond understanding, is Wenger's sturbbonsness and refusal to adapt to the changing Premier League environment, despite all indications that the league he's in now is not the same one he won the about ten years ago.

Now let's go ahead and take a look at some of those failures, if we may

  • Losing start players
  • Passing up opportunites to get star players when he can
  • Refusing to adapt to the changing Premier League
